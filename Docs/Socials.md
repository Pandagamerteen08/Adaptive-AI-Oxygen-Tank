# 🚧 Work In Progress

Please be patient with me while I continue building everything out as a solo developer. Some links, pages, projects, branding, community features, storefronts, and other parts of the studio may not be fully available yet while I continue learning, improving, and developing everything behind the scenes. I am currently balancing multiple projects at once, including Unreal Engine game development, a Subnautica mod project, social media management, learning new systems and workflows, and planning future releases for both Steam and Epic Games. Progress may sometimes seem slow from the outside, but work is constantly being done behind the scenes almost every day. Every page listed here will continue evolving over time as the studio grows, improves, and becomes more established. More updates, projects, announcements, and content are coming in the future as development continues.

I am also currently learning Unreal Engine Blueprints while actively developing projects at the same time. Alongside my Unreal Engine work, I am also developing a separate game project in Unity, although my main focus is currently Unreal Engine because I personally prefer the visual quality, graphical capabilities, atmosphere, and overall creative freedom it offers. Even though I am currently farther ahead in the Unity project, I want to dedicate more time toward Unreal Engine development because it aligns more closely with the kinds of experiences and visuals I ultimately want to create. I am learning as I go, which means some systems, mechanics, and workflows may take time to fully understand and polish correctly. I will also be honest and transparent about the fact that I use AI tools to help me better understand certain concepts, systems, and workflows faster. The goal is never to have AI build everything for me, but instead to use it as a learning tool so I can continue improving my own knowledge and skills over time. Learning while actively creating projects is part of the process, and I believe real experience comes from building things firsthand, making mistakes, solving problems, and continuing to improve step by step.

Fun fact: the game *Choo-Choo Charles* was created by solo developer Gavin Eisenbeisz, also known as Two Star Games. The project became widely known because it was developed primarily by one person using Unreal Engine Blueprints with very little C++ involved. The game itself was developed in Unreal Engine 4.27, and seeing projects like that is genuinely motivating because it proves that solo developers are capable of creating ambitious and successful games with enough time, dedication, patience, and persistence. Projects like that remind me that learning independently and building things step by step is completely possible, even when the process feels overwhelming at times.

If you are interested in becoming part of the journey, supporting the studio, or potentially helping with future projects, feel free to contact me through Discord, YouTube comments, Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, or email. I am always open to meeting creative, respectful, and supportive people who are passionate about game development, content creation, modding, art, storytelling, programming, community building, or simply supporting indie creators. Constructive feedback, encouragement, ideas, and genuine support are always appreciated and help more than most people probably realize. However, harassment, unnecessary negativity, spam, trolling, or disrespectful behavior may result in blocks or removal from community spaces. Please keep interactions respectful for everyone involved so the community can remain welcoming and enjoyable for everybody.
